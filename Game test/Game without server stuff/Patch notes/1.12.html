new

Auto load level scripts
With some PHP magic, the game now loads all the available levels automatically instead of having to add them manually to the Game test.php file.

new

Level builder
You can now build your own costum level without the need of expert coding from here

updated

Bullets and shots interactions
Bullets now expire if they exceed the origin range from where they were shot even if they origin has moved rathar than from where the origin currently is.

updated

Info and damage display system
Info system now displays the infos in all directions rather than only upwards. General optimazations on the system.


new

Glorious Executioner

  • A unit affected by the Glorious Executioner has its healing from all sources reduced by 40% (this includes health regeneration, lifesteal, revives, etc).
  • The Glorious Executioner debuff does not stack which means that a healing debuff of 40% is the maximum a unit can have.


Mortal reminder

removed No longer reduces the healing of all enemies in your range by 40%

new effect Now applies the Glorious Executioner to all enemies in your range


Essence Reaver

new effect Now removes the AD granted from its passive if the unit is above 50% maximum health.

new Now displays the AD granted when active on the unit's HUD


Bug fixes and other changes
  • Fixed bug where the amount of gold the player has earned after killing a monster wouldn't display
  • In-code item list has been completly redesigned and the itemsDataHolder property has been optimized and redesigned
