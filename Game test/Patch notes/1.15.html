Requiem

Requiem is a new ultimate ability that can be used by pressing R.

Upon triggering the ultimate, player roots himself and channels for 3 seconds, cursing all monsters in the current level applying The Glorious Executioner on them for 4 seconds. After the channel time has ended, player deals magic damage to all enemies cursed and applying on-hit effects.

Damage: 400 + 100% AP (This damage cannot critical strike)
Cooldown: 15 seconds (Cooldown starts upon triggering the ultimate)
