new

Lithality
Lithality is a flat amount of armor penetration that will be ignored from the target's armor when attacking it. Note: the percentage armor penetration applies before the flat armor penetration

For example if a the attacker has 100 AD, 20 lithality and the defender has 50 armor, the damage dealth will be 76.9 instead of 66.6. However, if the attacker has 20 | 15% lithality, the damage dealt will be instead 81.6 of 70.1 (because the target armor has been reduced to: 50 - 15% = 42.5 → 42.5 - 20 = 22.5)
