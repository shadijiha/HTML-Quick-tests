Hot fix

Guinsoo's Rageblade

Guinsoo's Rageblade was way too powerful against tank items. We are reducing the initial burn amount as well as making it deal physical damage instead of true damage. Also, we are extending the duration to give the chance to affected units to lifesteal. We are also adding a scaling on the burn maximum health damage to prevent source from building tank.

Damage type true physical
Burn damage 5% 4% + 1% per 100 bonus AD (capped at 100%)
Duration 3 seconds 5 seconds

Thornmail

Thornmail was still too weak even after patch 1.5 rework. So we are giving it some significant buffs.

removed No longer critical strikes based on owner critical strike chance.
